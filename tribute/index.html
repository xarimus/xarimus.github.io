This is a tribute

Actor, comedian, and rock star, Jack Black

Some notable moments in Jack Black's illustrious life and career

  • 1969 – Born in Santa Monica, California
  • 1982 – Appeared in commercial for the video game Pitfall!
  • 1994 – Formed comedic rock group Tenacious D with friend Kyle Gass
  • 2000 – Break out performance as Barry in High Fidelity
  • 2001 – Played Hal Larsen in Shallow Hal
  • 2001 – Released the self-titled album Tenacious D
  • 2003 – Played Dewey Finn in School of Rock for which he was nominated for a Golden Globe award
  • 2005 – Played Carl Denham in King Kong
  • 2006 – Played Miles in The Holiday
  • 2006 – Played Nacho in Nacho Libre
  • 2006 – Released the album The Pick of Destiny
  • 2006 – Played Jack "Jables" Black in Tenacious D in The Pick of Destiny
  • 2008 – Played the voice of Po in Kung Fu Panda
  • 2008 – Played Jeff "Fats" Portnoy in Tropic Thunder
  • 2011 – Played the voice of Po in Kung Fu Panda 2
  • 2011 – Played Bernie Tiede in Bernie for which he recevied his second Golden Globe nomination
  • 2012 – Released the album Rize of the Fenix
  • 2015 – Played/voiced multiple parts in Goosebumps
  • 2016 – Played the voice of Po in Kung Fu Panda 3
  • Currently – Still Rockin'
